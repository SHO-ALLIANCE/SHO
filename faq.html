FAQ - SHO Alliance

What is SHO Alliance?
SHO Alliance is a community of skilled players across multiple games, including Hitwicket, Free Fire, BGMI, E-Football, and Clash of Clans.
Which alliances are in Hitwicket?
VANGUARDS, ENFORCERS, OUTRIDERS, SENTINELS, PALADINS, CRUSADERS, REVENANTS, and INVINCIBLES.
Do we have alliances for other games?
Yes! FIREHAWKS for Free Fire, WARHOUNDS for BGMI, GLADIATORS for E-Football, and MASTERS for Clash of Clans.
